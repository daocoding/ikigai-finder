# Stage 2 — Talents → the *How*

**Goal:** surface how this person *naturally operates* — the things that are effortless for them and effort-full for others — rate them, and (on the full path) extract the reusable pattern behind their single strongest one.

A talent here is **not a skill**. Skills are learned and can fade; a talent is your **default way of being** — what you'd do even unpaid, what you can't help doing. The book's frame: *a talent is something you do so naturally you don't even notice it's special.*

## How to recognize a talent (use these as probes)

- What's been easy for you since childhood?
- What do others find hard that you do without effort?
- What do people **thank you** for?
- What were you **scolded for** as a kid? (Often a talent in disguise — "too talkative" → connects people; "too stubborn" → consistency.)
- What's a weakness of yours? **Flip it** — every shadow has a strength on the other side.
- What could you do for hours without getting tired?

## Run it

1. Ask the talents questions for their path from [`../questionnaires/fast.md`](../questionnaires/fast.md) / [`../questionnaires/full.md`](../questionnaires/full.md). One at a time; reflect back.
2. For each answer, name the **talent underneath the story** — the verb, the pattern — and map it to the official talents in [`../reference/talents-100.md`](../reference/talents-100.md). Every entry there pairs a **strength with its shadow**; **name both**. Saying "your gift for "X also shows up as 'Y when you're stretched" builds trust and is itself part of the self-understanding.
3. Collect **5–8 talents.**

## Rate each talent: ◎ / ○ / △

Self-rating sharpens the synthesis later. For each talent, ask the user which it is:

| Symbol | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **◎** | Energizing **and** tied to real results you've actually produced |
| **○** | Energizing — feels like you, even if results are still forming |
| **△** | Unsure — might be a talent, needs testing |

In synthesis you'll lead with the **◎** talents. Record the rating next to each talent.

## Full path only — the 8-angle deep dive

Take their **single strongest talent** (a ◎) and run the **8 angles** (in [`../questionnaires/full.md`](../questionnaires/full.md)) against a specific time they felt deeply fulfilled using it. The 8 angles walk through: what led up to it → the environment → the specific actions → the thinking behind them → what they noticed → their motivation → when the fulfillment faded and how to protect it → what they'd do better.

The payoff: you move from "I'm good at helping people" (vague) to **the reusable mechanism** — "I help people by asking the one question that makes them hear themselves." That mechanism is what you'll plug into the synthesis.

## Reveal & visualize

Show your reasoning per talent: the **source story → the talent → why that rating** (*"'three juniors finally understood the system' → making the complex approachable; ◎ because it energized you AND produced a result"*). Then render a **tiered talents map** with the ◎/○/△ tiers as branches so the strongest read first. Recipe in [visuals.md](visuals.md).

## Before moving on

Summarize their rated talents (◎ first) and, if full path, the one-line mechanism from the deep dive. Get a "yes, that's how I work." Then go to [03-love.md](03-love.md).
