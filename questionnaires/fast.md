# Fast questionnaire (~15 min)

5 questions per pillar, 15 total. Ask **one at a time**, in the user's language (translate these as needed — they're written in English, but run the conversation in whatever language the user uses). Reflect each answer back before moving on. Run them in order: Values → Talents → Love. The method for each stage is in [`../method/`](../method/).

---

## 1. Values — 5 questions
→ then derive the **Work Purpose**. See [`../method/01-values.md`](../method/01-values.md).

1. Who do you respect (a friend, a public figure, a character)? What *specifically* do you respect about them?
2. What experiences in your childhood or adolescence had the biggest impact on you? How did they shape your values?
3. What do you feel is missing or wrong in society today?
4. Ask someone close to you: *"What do you think I value most in life?"* — what did they say?
5. When raising a child or advising someone, what is the #1 thing you'd most want to tell them? What would you most *not* want to tell them?

---

## 2. Talents — 5 questions
→ rate each ◎ / ○ / △ (defined in the method file). See [`../method/02-talents.md`](../method/02-talents.md).

1. In your life so far, what experience felt the most fulfilling or rewarding?
2. What has recently made you feel irritated, restless, or anxious?
3. Ask someone close to you: *"What do you think my strengths are?"* — what did they say?
4. If you quit your job tomorrow, what parts would you miss? (No job? Think of a past one.)
5. What results have you achieved in the past, and *exactly how* did you achieve them?

---

## 3. Love — 5 questions
→ map to domains. See [`../method/03-love.md`](../method/03-love.md).

1. What is something you'd happily pay money to study right now?
2. What kinds of books are on your bookshelf?
3. Have you ever encountered a field or thing that made you feel *"This is amazing!"* or *"This saved me!"*?
4. In your life so far, what work did you want to say "thank you" for? (Or: who did you want to thank?)
5. What social issues make you feel angry?

---

After all 15: proceed to **Synthesis** ([`../method/04-synthesis.md`](../method/04-synthesis.md)) and then **Means** ([`../method/05-means.md`](../method/05-means.md)). The fast path uses the same synthesis and means as the full path.
