# Reference — Interest Domains

Use this in Stage 3 ([`../method/03-love.md`](../method/03-love.md)) as a controlled vocabulary for naming the *fields* that pull the user. 100 domains; the 12 groups below are a navigational map, with the complete list after. Anchor, not cage — blends ("the psychology of money") are fine and often the most alive. (Running in another language? Translate as you go.)

## Quick map — 12 groups

- **Nature & Environment** — Animals, Flowers, Agriculture, Forestry, Space, Nature, Outdoors
- **Technology** — Robots, IT, Computers, Electronics, Video Production
- **Creative Arts** — Art, Photography, Product Design, Graphic Design, Music, Poetry, Instruments, Literature
- **Entertainment & Media** — Entertainment, Acting, Movies, TV, Books, Magazines, Games, Anime, Manga, Show Biz
- **Health & Wellness** — Sports, Exercise, Medical Tech, Rehab, Medicine, Nutrition, Medical Care, Nursing, Paramedic
- **Travel & Lifestyle** — Travel, Tourism, Theme Parks, Hotels, Fashion, Beauty, Relaxation, Lifestyle
- **Food & Drink** — Cooking, Sweets, Alcohol, Food, Diet
- **Business & Finance** — Finance, Business, Career, Management, Real Estate, Marketing, Economics, Sales, Consulting
- **Education & Social** — Education, Childcare, Social Welfare, Psychology, Philosophy, Religion
- **Design & Engineering** — Architecture, Civil Engineering, Interior Design, Cars, Planes, Ships, Trains
- **Law & Politics** — Politics, Law, Administration, Security
- **Relationships** — Family, Romance, Marriage, Weddings
