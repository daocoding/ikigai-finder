# Reference — 100 Talents (each with its strength & shadow)

Use this in Stage 2 ([`../method/02-talents.md`](../method/02-talents.md)) as a controlled vocabulary for naming talents. The point of the **shadow** column: every talent, overused or stressed, shows up as a weakness — naming *both* sides is how the user recognizes the talent as truly theirs (and it's more honest than flattery).

> Running the journey in another language? Translate each talent name and its strength/shadow on the fly — you're more than capable — and treat the English table below as canonical.
